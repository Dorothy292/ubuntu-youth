What We Offer

After-School Tutoring

After-school tutoring is a vital component of the educational system, providing students with the support and resources they need to succeed academically. Tutors work with students to identify their individual learning needs and goals, and develop and implement customized instruction plans to help them achieve those goals.

Career and Skills Workshops

Coaching workshops will empower you with the skills, knowledge and confidence to find and prepare for your next job or career move. Group workshops will be available both online and face-to-face with small groups. Some of the areas we will cover include:

  • Clarifying what you want
  • Perfecting your CV/Application form
  • Job search and application strategies
  • Presenting your best LinkedIn profile
  • Interview preparation (including online interviews)
  • Your personal brand
  • Salary & Benefits negotiation

Mentorship & Life Coaching

Mentorship and life coaching are two distinct yet complementary approaches to personal and professional development. A mentor is an experienced individual who provides guidance, inspiration, and support, often sharing their wisdom and insights based on their personal experiences. They help set and achieve personal and professional goals, offer encouragement, and provide reassurance during challenging times.

Holiday Programs and Community Outreach

Holiday programs and community outreach focus on providing essential services and support to underserved populations during the festive season. These programs aim to extend help by offering vital resources and information, particularly during events like Christmas.
